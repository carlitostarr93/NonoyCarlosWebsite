Who we are

Passion, technique
and heart

NonoyCarlos Bachata was born from the union of two artists with a single mission:
to transmit the soul of bachata with rigor, authenticity, and a lot of feeling.

Our Passion for Fusion
a solid base that fuses styles

Our proposal stems from over a decade of dancing and an international career touring workshops and stages as a professional couple. We have designed our own style that merges the essence of bachata with the strength of urban dance and the fluidity of bachazouk.

As certified instructors by referents like Korke & Judith and Masa & Polina, our mission is to provide an elite methodology adapted to your pace, where technical rigor and enjoyment go hand in hand.
