Privacy Policy (GDPR)

1. Data Controller

The responsible entity for your data is the association Bachata Maestra, contactable at bachatamaestra@gmail.com.

2. Data Collection and Purpose

We only process data that you voluntarily provide through direct messages (Instagram), emails, or feedback forms. The purposes are:

  • Improving our activities through review analysis.

3. Data Retention and Security

Data is kept as long as necessary for association management and legal compliance. We do not share your data with third parties outside the association and its internal volunteers necessary for the activity.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your data at any time by emailing bachatamaestra@gmail.com.
