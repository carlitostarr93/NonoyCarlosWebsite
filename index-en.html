Who we are

More than a decade
of passion and technique

Our proposal stems from over a decade of dancing and an international career touring workshops and stages as a professional couple. We have designed our own style that merges the essence of bachata with the strength of urban dance and the fluidity of sensual bachata and bachazouk.

Based in Barcelona and certified by global referents of the genre, we deliver a high-performance teaching level tailored to each student — regardless of their starting point.
