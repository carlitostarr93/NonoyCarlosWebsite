About Us

Our Passion
for Fusion

Our approach stems from over a decade of dancing and a year of international experience, touring workshops and stages as a professional couple.

We have designed our own style that blends the essence of bachata with the energy of urban dance and the fluidity of sensual and bachazouk — creating an artistic proposition that connects with audiences across the globe.
