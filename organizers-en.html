About Us

Our Passion
for Fusion

Our approach is born from over a decade of dancing and a year of international experience touring workshops and stages as a professional couple.

We have designed our own style that merges the essence of bachata with the power of urban dance and the fluidity of sensual and bachazouk — creating an artistic proposition that connects with audiences across all latitudes.

We understand how a festival works from the inside: organizers need artists who add value. That's why we don't hide in the backstage; we dance on the floor with your attendees until closing time, keeping the energy high until the very end.
